# Mobile-Sale-Dashbord-Power-BI-
📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

An interactive Power BI Dashboard designed to analyze mobile phone sales performance across multiple dimensions, including sales trends, customer ratings, payment methods, brands, cities, and time periods.

🚀 Features
📊 Sales Overview
> Total Sales Revenue
> Total Quantity Sold
> Average Customer Rating
> Total Transactions
📈 Sales Analysis
> Month-to-Date (MTD) sales tracking
> Year-over-Year (YoY) comparison
> Same Period Last Year (SPLY) analysis
> Sales trend by day, month, quarter, and year
🌍 Geographic Insights
> City-wise sales distribution using interactive maps
> Regional performance comparison
📱 Product Performance
> Mobile model-wise sales analysis
> Brand-wise sales and quantity comparison
> Top-performing products identification
💳 Payment Analysis
> Transaction breakdown by payment method:
> UPI
> Credit Card
> Debit Card
> Cash
⭐ Customer Insights
> Customer rating distribution
> Rating status segmentation (Good, Average, Poor)
🎛 Interactive Filters
> Mobile Model Filter
> Brand Filter
> Payment Method Filter
> Year/Quarter/Month Selection
> Dynamic drill-down capabilities
🛠 Tools & Technologies
> Power BI Desktop
> DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
> Power Query
> Data Modeling
> Interactive Visualizations
📷 Dashboard Pages
1. Dashboard Overview
Provides a complete view of sales KPIs, city-wise sales, payment methods, customer ratings, and product performance.

2. MTD Report
Tracks Month-to-Date sales growth and daily performance trends.

3. Same Period Last Year Analysis
Compares current sales performance against the same period from the previous year to identify growth patterns and business trends.

🎯 Business Value
This dashboard helps stakeholders:
> Monitor overall sales performance
> Identify top-performing brands and models
> Analyze customer satisfaction
>Track sales growth over time
>Make data-driven business decisions
